# Take Ground
Luke 19:1-10
## Power to be a Witness
"When you find your people, you find your purpose"
*Spiritual action without the heart accomplishes little, and is better than if you just don't do it*
It has nothing to do with "personality type", it has to do with insecurity, anxiety, social awkwardness
70-85% of people who attend a church for the first time because they were invited by a friend or coworker
2% of Christians invite an unchurched person
82% would attend if invited by a friend
7 out of 10 have never been invited
Insecurity, fear of altering the relationship, awkwardness and a lack of faith
Matthew 28:18-20 and surely, I am with you until the end of the age
For all of us, the standard is to go and make disciples
Acts 1:8
*How do I reach others?*
1. Notice
It is a yielding to see the people around you
When you get closer to the Holy Spirit, He always leads you towards people. Your coworker. Your lost friend. Your friend you haven't reached out to in a while.
In the business, you can miss that God put someone in your path
One of the things you can pray is "show me the people around me that don't know you"
2. Seek God
Jesus noticed Zaccheus and went to him
As a seasoned believer, it can be easy to forget the why
Every time you get closer to God, you will get a bigger heart for people, as that is what is on His heart
*There is no shortage of need and people to help*
When you ask God for His power, there will be a power that will come over you
3. Step Out
When you receive the power of the Holy Spirit, many think of gifts, externals, but it is mainly the boldness, being a witness of the power and grace of God
