What do I do?

1.  Pray out loud, release the offense, give it to Jesus, and ask for His peace.

2.  Write down the unforgiven offense on a piece of paper- tear up the paper or burn it as a physical demonstration of release.

3.  Tell a trusted voice- confession

4.  If you're able, pray a nlessing over the person you forgave.

5.  If this is an ongoing relationship, prayerfully clarify your boundaries.
