Give Thanks

1 Thessalonians 5:18

*In everything give thanks; for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus for you*

**Giving thanks multiplies, overcomes a scarcity mindset**

How we tend to think:

- “I'll be grateful when \*blank\* happens”

- “I can't be grateful because of \*blank\*”

- “I don't feel grateful - I don't want to be fake.”

  - People keep trying to play emotional tetris, everything must be neat and tidy

- “How did my life end up here?”

Psalm 107:1 Oh, give thanks to the Lord, for He is good! For His mercy endures forever.

In the storm is when you find out how much He loves you…

Why is it so important to give thanks?

1.  Giving thanks filters our thoughts

Philippians 1:3

2.  Giving thanks is the foundation for generosity

We want to feel generous without actually being generous

We can do that by being thankful for what He has given us

2 Corinthians 9:7

3.  Giving thanks brings us close to God.
