Live Lighter

Overcoming Everything That Weighs Us Down

Psalm 55:22 Cast your burden on the Lord, and He will sustain you

We all have burdens- things that weigh us down. You're going to cast your burdens or carry them.

*Why do we feel heavy?*

1.  We have an insatiable desire for more.

What is burnout? Workplace stress that hasn't been addressed.

We are told by Jesus that we will have trials and tribulations.

John 16:33 in this world you will have trouble. But take heart! I have overcome this world.

2.  We have burdens, trials, challenges and tribulations

3.  Our culture over-emphasizes the externals

4.  We havent discovered that Jesus offers a better way

Jesus is not someone who is neutral

Jesus demands a response

Matthew 11:28-30 take my yoke upon you… for my yoke is easy and my burden is light

Jesus says “ill offer you what ive already done” the life He lived on your behalf that you never earned or deserved

2 Corinthians 5:21

**You feel guilty because you are guilty. Take this exchange and you become righteous.**

How does Jesus help us live lighter?

1.  We realize the weight isn't the problem- it's how we're carrying it.

It isnt about what you do, but how you carry it

Bigger isnt just stronger, bigger can just be bigger. Its not about the weight, its about how you carry it

You can lift a small amount of weight and hurt your back

Wrong ways to carry weight

1.  Pride

2.  Comparison

3.  Identity

Some identify with what they are carrying.

“One handful with peace vs a handful with stress”

Right way to carry weight

1.  Im stewarding this for Jesus.

2.  He will help me carry it.

Your soul is dead if you don't have the Holy Spirit

We dont see long term change from external pressure, there has to be an inward change
