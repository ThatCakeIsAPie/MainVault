Vision Weekend

Vision comes from God.

<u>Where am I in regard to vision?</u>

1.  Vision-less

Vision leaks; it drifts… i lost it!

2.  Vision-scarred

Hurt, let down, or disappointed

3.  Vision-fatigued

Chasing multiple visions but not accomplishing any

Disintegrate- break up into small parts, typically as the result of impact or decay

It's the enemies desire to disintegrate God's vision for us

We try to keep a list on our lives, like we are compartmentalized beings

Integrity- To integrate

Integrate God into your everyday life

In every situation, ask “God, what is your vision for me in this situation?”

**This weekend I want to make the vision of Jesus clear, compelling, and something we're all moving toward.**

**Not “nice”, but vital.**

**Not only “what”, but why.**

Dont become religious, it makes you become mad at God.

Acts 26:15-19 Appoint you as a servant and as a witness of what you have seen and will see of me… To open their eyes and turm them from darkness to light… “I was not disobedient to the vision from heaven.”

*You cant forgive yourself, you have to be forgiven*

How do we integrate our lives into God's vision?

1.  We keep coming back to God's original vision. (Commit)

2.  We all have a part in God's vision (Contribute)

3.  We remember God's vision impacts eternity (Care)

If you committed to Jesus Christ, *you signed up*

We don't need a new vision, we need to re-commit to the original vision.

*No one else can give you your contribution*

Ephesians 4:16 builds itself up in Love, *as each part does its work*.

**Love is the highest motivator**

***If you care, it moves you to action***

You clap during a baptism because it means it is one more person who is going to live in eternity.

**If you want vision, you have to have an eternal perspective**

<u>God's vision always stretches us beyond our human abilities</u>

“Let the eyes of your heart be open to the calling God has.”
