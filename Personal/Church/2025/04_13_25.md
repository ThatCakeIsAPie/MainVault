How does Jesus’ purpose work?

Tell your kids that they can be everything God wants them to be, not do anything you want to be

1.  He works *in* us to work *through* us.

Do you know enough to show someone you love them?

2.  He's with you even when you cant see Him.

Matthew 28:20 “And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.”

We like acting like we know because it makes us feel safe.

When you dont know what He's doing, you can say He has forgotten you, or you can trust that He is working in the background.

“Availability bias” you default to what you have been listening to.

In your pain and struggle, you can lean into Him, or away from Him.

3.  His purpose always results in lost people coming home.

Easter is a celebration of Jesus. We dont come to Him, he lowers Himself to come to us, riding a donkey.

He comes to those who feel the furthest from Him.

Someone in your life needs Jesus.

What if it is your neighbor, your loved one?
