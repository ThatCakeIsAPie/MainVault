<u>How Do I Partner with Jesus to Live Free from Worry?</u>

1.  Process your worries with Him.

**The root of worry is control. We are control freaks.**

How do I partner with Jesus to live free from worry?

Control and anxiety are connected. The more you increase it, the more anxiety. The more you trust, the more anxiety goes down.

<img src="media/image1.jpg" style="width:6.5in;height:3in" />

Luke 10:40-42

Being burdened isnt even always about what you are doing, but how you are doing

2.  Remember who you are.

If you find identity in your work, its not meant to be your identity.

It transcends your understanding.

3.  Increase faith-building exercises.

We have everything available to raise your unbelief

We all have that one person in our life that knows all the bad in the world, every scandal. No wonder why we are all worried?

**When worry and anxiety comes, you have to get aggressive.**

Worry and anxiousness are contagious. But guess what? ***Faith is too.***

4.  Practice gratitude.

Lost people, who dont know Jesus, know this.

**All truth is God's truth. The world is just stumbling on what God already said.**

The more you practice gratitude, the more your heart aligns with what is true

5.  Be more intentional about exposure.

You are going to have to put up some more fences than other people.

He has had reasons to be jaded, but he just doesn't listen to them. He doesnt listen to scandals, because he doesnt need it in his spirit.

He doesnt have FOMO, he has JOMO, Joy of Missing Out

Mind your eye gates and ear gates

You become more like the people you hang around

**I have things that are making me feel anxious, but I am not an anxious person.**

6.  Avoid isolation.

The enemy wants you isolated so he can beat you up.

When you come to Jesus, you feel lighter.
