**Freedom** 9-week small group w/ Freedom weekend after

Discipleship is to take your next step, and help others take their next step

We do small groups to;

- Take care of people's needs

- Help people connect

- Help people grow

We all want to grow… *you wouldnt be here if you didnt want to*

That is discipleship

What is a disciple?

One Christ-follower taking their own steps, helping another take their own steps

It is not a class or an exchange of information… it is a seed that never stops growing

You will grow the most when you walk with others on this journey.

There will be others with you, ahead of you, and behind you.

It is not just about you, taking steps with others, having others pour into you, but you pouring into others.

You can always disciple someone, because there is always someone behind you.

Simply just inviting someone else, you can help disciple.

*The Great Co-mission* Matthew 28:18-20

- God has called you to be a disciple

- God has called you to make a disciple

Jesus put His entire mission on this strategy.

You teach what you know

You reproduce what you know

You commit to Jesus, and help others commit too

You commit to receiving coaching with a good attitude

You have to be a disciple to make a disciple. You have to be one to make one.

1.  Look around you

2.  Be with them

3.  Help them with the basics

4.  Challenge and encourage them
