Authentic: Week 3

Trusted Voices

Knows you.

Not just your name and where you work, but knows your life.

There are a lot of voices that want your attention. Not all voices can be trusted.

76% of people think a trusted voice is important, but only 37% have one.

Hebrews 13:7 “Consider the outcome of their way of life and imitate their faith.”

Acts 9:10-18

Trusted voices help us see what we cannot see

Galatians 2:1-2 Saul meets with trusted voices

He wasnt just waiting for it to happen, he took ownership of it

**What keeps us from seeking trusted voices?**

Barriers vs God's Best

- Ignorance - I dont see the value

  - Wisdom - Trusted voices protect me from failure

- Fear - I will be held back from my potential

  - Faith - Trusted voices help me go farther than I can alone

- Pride - I want to do what I want to do

  - Humility - Trusted voices guide me when I can't see what is best

- Pain - I've been hurt in the past

  - Trust - God will restore and provide relationships

**What can I do this week?**

1.  Identify a trusted voice

2.  Invite their feedback

3.  Listen and apply

“*Our problem isn't access - it's invitation*”
