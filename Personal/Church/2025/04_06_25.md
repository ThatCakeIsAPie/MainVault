Resurrection: Week Two- Power

“You will know they are my disciples, not by the way they talk, but by the way they love.”

There is a power that is not dependent on human technology. But the power of the Heavenly Father can move and make wonders occur.

Week three will be about Purpose

***<u>The problem isnt actually usually a sight or evidence based problem, rather, a heart problem based on previous pain.</u>***

You dont have a bible understanding problem, you have a surrender problem

“Stay in the city until you have been clothed with power from on high.”

1.  We have His peace

We cant create it. We have to accept it from Him.

In His final moments, he kept reiterating “im going to give you a peace that the world cannot take away”

2.  We can see genuine transformation.

He didnt ask you to “check a box”

He asked you to surrender to Him and receive Him

***<u>If you say you accepted Jesus Christ, and you havent had a radical transformation, you havent, you just joined a religion.</u>***

***<u>You feel different because you are different.</u>***

When you experience Jesus, a stone rolls off your heart

3.  We give and receive forgiveness (for your sins and those who sinned against you).

We tend to not have an acute awareness of where we would have been without His grace

You have to push away from the flesh

4.  We have the power to live it.

***<u>If you want to have true conviction, see what He is doing in your life, and testify what you have seen.</u>*** Stop trying to do it for what has happened for others.
