MYA 2nd Preview Service

We’ve all been given something we dont know what to do with

Jesus is trying to call a principle that we are called to live

The expectation is that when you are given something, you return it better than you got it.

God has blessed you with abundance, but He is looking to see what you are going to do with what you are given

Your assignment + faithful stewardship = multiplied blessings

What do i need to steward?

1.  Your relationships

Everything comes after your relationship with Jesus, it all flows after

Have you shared what God has been doing in your life with a friend recently? Not to condemn them, but to remind them

2.  Your time

You can become swamped: what are you saying yes to that is getting in the way of your relationship with God

3.  Your money

Proverbs 3:9-10

4.  Your growth

We are called to step out and use the gift that God gave you

How do i steward well?

1.  Focus on what's in front of you

Dont miss what is right in front of you. You can look back and think “why did i want to get out of that season so fast?”

You wont get to the next step without focusing on where you are at

You want the results without the process, we want to skip steps. But you cant circumvent the process that God has you in.

It is about what God intends for you.

1 Peter 4:10

2.  Focus on your motivation

He isnt focused on your externals, but your motivation

Colossians 3:23-24

The rightful motivation with faithful stewardship, you will be shocked where you end up

3.  Focus on your heart

Romans 14:12

4.  Focus on others

How can I love the person who is right next to me?

Pray for them

How you steward the little things is how you steward everything

If you dont steward your car well, how can you be expected to steward anything else well?

God cares what your room looks like

If every relationship has problems, its not a them thing, its a you thing.
