- We think bigger vision; God's building bigger hearts

<u>Barriers That Keep Us From Giving</u>

Galatians 5:7-8

1.  We cant see the relationship between grace and giving

*Giving out of guilt doesnt count. It has to come from a cheerful heart*

It is not a “i have to” but a “i want to”

Let's not lose sight of the grace of God

2.  We cant balance the sovereignty of God with faith

You can easily mistake the fact that God has it covered, and walking it faith

3.  We give ourselves an exemption

Almost always, the more you have, the more afraid you are of losing what you have.
