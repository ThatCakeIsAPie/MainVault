Christmas at MYA

Luke 2:8-20

God sees the unseen, pursues those the world ignores

The shepherds were the first people to receive God's invitation

What did Jesus come to bring?

1.  Eternal Hope

The amount of hopelessness found in Christians in this age group is wild…

2.  Everlasting Peace

Because He died for our sins, he brought peace to our lives, knowing that even in the end, we are good

He doesn't just bring peace, He is the Prince of Peace

3.  Enduring Joy

*The invitation is waiting, but a response is required*
