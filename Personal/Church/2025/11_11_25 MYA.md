Make a Difference

Stewardship lies between foolishness and fear

We can find ourselves in foolishness, not driven by God's word and plan, but in their own emotions

Stewardship is a test of your heart

There are areas in your life that God has come up again and again

How do I better steward what God has given me?

1.  Take Inventory

Most people dont actually know where they are actually at

*You cant manage what you wont acknowledge*

Take account with what God has given you

Luke 16:10 Whoever can be trusted with little can be trusted with much

Always know where your finances are at

2.  Be Diligent

Diligence = Discipline x Intentionality

*Discipline isn't restriction, it's direction*

The way you steward the small things is the way you steward everything

**It is a character thing**

If you dont return the tithe, you are robbing God

I will not keep what is God

*Ive never seen someone obey God and not get blessed*

Have you built your life in a way in which you can be generous?

3.  Live Purposefully

*Resources are the fuel behind what God has given us*

It is about sending your resources towards purpose

*Stewardship always tests your heart*

How well are you taking care of the things that God has given you to steward?
