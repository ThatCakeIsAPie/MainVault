MYA is not a gathering of people, we are a family

“What can I do to make sure our marriage lasts?”

“Is my life going to matter?”

“I have a lot of acquaintances, but there are times I feel really lonely.”

This isnt an MYA idea, it was God's idea

Psalm 68:6 God sets the lonely in families

Ruth 1:16-17 Where you are going, I am going.

There is something about the conviction you have with the people in your life that has a profound effect on the backend of your life

The conviction someone had, God used to restore not only a natural family, but the Savior of humanity came from that lineage

Two Truths about Spiritual Family:

1.  You dont know what it is until you experience it

God leads the rebellious into sun-scorched land

If you dont get settled, you rebel and end up in that land

God has a family for you

<u>What are some barriers that prevent you from leaning in just a bit more</u>?

- Natural Family

Regardless of your background, God wants to set you in a family

In the isolation of not wanting to have God's best for you, you can blaze a trail of loneliness.

It is hard to have relationships when all you do is rebel

- Church Hurt

You'll never let yourself get deep with someone because of past hurt. It is easy to be shallow and transactional, because you are afraid of getting hurt again

In a spiritual family, you have mentors who are ahead of you. God places moms and dads in your life. It is one of the best things you can have in your life.

You have brothers and sisters, your peers, your ride-or-die people.

How cool is it to have people who are just your people?

You have sons and daughters, people who are behind you.

2.  When you find your people, you find your purpose

When you dont find your people, do you find your purpose?

It wasn't until the conviction of God was placed on his life, that God had a family for him, he understood where he was meant

*You can hear from God and still end up in a sun-scorched land*

Relationships take work. Once you get the desire in your heart, you have to take action.

Spiritual family is what MYA is built on. If you call this home, you are a part of it.

When you spend your whole life sowing seed in the relational bank account, you will have no idea what will happen when you need help.

*You are looking for your purpose, but have you found your people?*
