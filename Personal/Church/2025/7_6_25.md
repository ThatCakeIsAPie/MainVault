<u>Summer at Milestone</u>

His family went on a vacation, and they thought they would get recharged, but it didn't happen. He didn't read his Bible, and felt a bit more empty spiritually.

Proverbs 13:4 The soul of the sluggard craves and gets nothing, while the soul of the diligent is richly supplied.

We can get to our season of rest and end up feeling more depleted after if we aren't careful.

*How do I grow through and not just go through another summer?*

1 Timothy 4:14-16 Practice, immerse, progress, persist

Fixed Mindset vs. Growth Mindset

<img src="media/image1.jpg" style="width:6.5in;height:3in" />

Growth Cycle:

Practice -\> Immerse -\> Progress -\> Persist

1.  Grow in God

2 Peter 3:18 But grow in the grace and knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

2.  Grow in Relationships

Hebrews 10:24-25 And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, noy giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing.

3.  Grow in Purpose

1 Timothy 4:16 Persist in this, for by so doing you will save both yourself and your hearers.
