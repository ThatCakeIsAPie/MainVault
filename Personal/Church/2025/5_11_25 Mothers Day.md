When you face storms, what do you focus on? *What you focus on you become full of.* Are you consumed by your fear, or are you filled with Jesus?

Mark 6:45-53

1.  We understand that God orders our steps.

3.  God meets us in the middle.

    1.  Everything we do in life is in the middle.

    2.  Your story is what you choose to do in the middle.

    3.  You are creating your story. Live intentionally.

4.  God brings us to the other side.

God works all things. We may go through stuff, but he doesn't leave us in the middle.
