Taming the Tongue

James 1:26 If you claim to be religious but dont control your tongue, you are fooling yourself and your religion is worthless

1.  My words can determine my life's direction

James 3:3-4 Horses are controlled with a small bit in their mouths. Our mouth controls us.

People can speak life over us “God's got great things planned for you”

*The tongue has the power of life and death*

**We shape our words and then our words shape us**

2.  My words have power to destroy what I have

James 3:5-6

A mans teacher cursed him, an executive at Exxon turned it around

3.  My words reveal what's in my heart

James 3:9-10 Blessing the father, cursing men with the same tongue

**The real problem isnt the tongue, it is the heart**

James 3:7-8 All creatures can be tamed, but no human being can tame the tongue

1.  Allow God to change my heart

*Christianity is not learned behavior, a religion, but the moment God recreates you and you are a new creation*

Hebrews 8:10-12

2.  Put a filter on what I allow in my heart

Holy means set apart from the way the world lives

Matthew 12:34-35

Proverbs 4:23 Above all else, guard your heart, for everything you do flows from it

*Expose the deeds of darkness*

3.  Speak words of life

What would it look like if you went on mission, and spoke words that bless people

- Words of Affection

Matthew 3:16-17

God would tell you He loves you, not talk about what you did wrong, if He was standing right in front of you

Tell your loved ones you love them

“You are my son, I love you, and I'm so proud of you”

- Words of Praise

Proverbs 25:11 A word aptly spoken is like apples of gold in settings of silver

- Words of Encouragement

Ephesians 4:29 Dont let any unwholesome talk come out, but build others up

Take 15 seconds to say to someone “thinking of you, i know you are going through a tough time”

- Words of Healing

Proverbs 15:4 The tongue that brings healing is a tree of life

Christians grieve, but not life that

How do we have hope?

- Words of Faith

Romans 4:17 We speak the obvious, and speak the things that aren't obvious

*Speak to their potential*

God knows what you did, you know what you did, but that doesn't matter.

Speak things that aren't, but will
