What is blocking the fruit? You can become an accidental pharisee/religious spirit

Mark 11:20-21

Three times the bible describes Israel as a fig tree with no fruit. Jeremiah 8, Micah 7, Isaiah 34

Cursing the fig tree is connected to cleansing the temple which would be destroyed in AC 70

Fig leaves are what Adam and Eve used to cover their shame in the Garden

Fig leaves are used as a symbol of man's attempt to cover their inadequacies

<u>What does this look like in our lives?</u>

- You may have heard a pastor say “religion vs relationship”

Religion is your attempt to “do”

- You focus more on the way things appear than the way they really are

- You emphasize outward piety over inward transformation

God's plan for you is Holy Matrimony

- You major on the minors - Jesus said “you strain out a gnat but swallow a camel”

Grace by itself leaves no rules and hurts people more

- You make personal convictions moral absolutes

- You're obnoxious but call it “boldness”

- *You disregard lost people, and you don't even attempt to relate to them*

**Jesus was and is the savior of the world**

Everything else is secondary. Every head and knee must bow.

His top identifier is he is a follower of Jesus Christ

*The main things esteemed in the bible are not what is esteemed in the world*

Kindness, forgiveness, loving

2.  Stay aware of common traps

“Hypocrites/white-washed tombs”

Good fruit- You have a genuine relationship with Jesus

The enemy is always talking about you to everyone else

Ask Jesus to keep you humble

“Brood of vipers”

Good fruit- You're a person of strong conviction, but you're also thoughtful and kind toward all people.

You can have these strong convictions, but still have a kind demeanor

You have to work extra hard to build influence with people. If they arent asking “tell me more”, then shut up, because you haven’t built enough relational capital to tell them

“Blind guides”

Good fruit- You don't demand that people live with convictions you can't consistently meet
