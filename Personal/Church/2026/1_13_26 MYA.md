3 Fundamentals to Having a Faith that Lasts

1.  Discipline Yourself to Trust God

**Faith is not an emotion, but a choice**

Usually, when the pain comes, you haven't actually trusted God in the way you should

2.  Renew your Mind

2 Corinthians 3:17-18

“The greatest strategy of the enemy is to give you situations to compromise on your character”

It isnt that you are making a bunch of mistakes, you dont have a lot of accountability in your life

If you capitalize your entire lifestyle to not have roots, it creates a way in which you have no accountability in your life

Renew your mind through the word, your daily bread, confessing the word over your life

Its not a magic trick, a condition

Check how you are talking

People of faith watch their confession over themselves

When it is living and active, it comes out
