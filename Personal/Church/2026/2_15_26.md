Fruitful

The fruit of the spirit is the evidence that you have surrendered to the spirit of God

The fruit is not just for your tree, but what other people can eat

If it is not a health concern, it is usually a relational issue.

“You tell me you are so spiritual, so holy, but your relationships are a dumpster fire”

Paul said that the fruit is what the world saw as “lower class” values

In today's climate, can you love imperfect people for an extended period of time?

You see from where you sit

The spirit of God gives you awareness that there is another person in another seat that has different experiences and backgrounds, not just allowing you to see from their point of view, but from God's.

Your young child needs a parent, not a friend

When they turn 13, you go from parent to coach, but they need to choose to let you be in consultation with them

You hurt their ability to relate with others if you make them feel as though they are the center of the world.

Galatians 5:14-15 For the entire law is fulfilled in keeping this one command: Love your neighbor as yourself. If you bite and devour each other, watch out as you consume each other.

Loving your neighbor is evidence you love your God

The fruit of the spirit is zigging when they think you should be zagging

*The biggest sign of going by the flesh is isolation*

The next sign is when they stop listening, they stop being coachable, stop considering a different point of view

They learn to emotionally manipulate to get their way, they respond out of hurt, “they'll take advantage of me”, building a platform instead of building people

If you do not teach your kid through discipline, which takes discipline, you hate your kid

The kid rebelling is not the one you need to worry about, but the one who is quiet, retracting, who needs their heart changed

We have so much division because microphones are being given to people freely, when you used to have to build relational equity to be given one.

When they are building their own platform, they think everyone is there to serve them, not for that person to serve them.

Good fruit is unity with God/others

The flesh is disunity

Where God sees unity, He commands a blessing

The number one thing to do for a kid is build stability with your spouse

The fruit of Christ is so transformative if you have kindness

Can you have friends for 25-30 years? Can you stay married to the same person?

There is a concern amongst younger generations that we are getting disconnected. It is hard for people to find friends nowadays.

It is not about everyone else's response, but to train them in the fruit of the spirit.
