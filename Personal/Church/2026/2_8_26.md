What kind of fruit does God grow?

Love, joy and peace

*Love is more than what God does, it's who He is*

“We fell out of love” is a worldly view

A love in marriage is one that comes from the Spirit. You have to start going first, with a love not based on what they did.

Joy - lifts our spirits, gives us the power to endure adversity, and fills us with hope.

The peace of God - Whole, complete, lacking nothing

Don't you just love it when your kids do things without pressure?

Dont you think how God feels when His people willingly yield to His Spirit?

“I didnt think I could do it” is what you hear from today's leaders. But they all did it anyway, despite their doubts. It is easy to doubt, but dont yield to your doubts, keep doing it anyway.

Today, you get recognized for being in the flesh.

You can recognize good fruit. Does it taste like His good fruit?

2\. Good fruit requires integration, not fragmentation.

Integrity comes from integer, meaning whole.

Be the same person you are at home, everywhere

You cant operate in the boardroom, and treat people in a different way, and say “it's just business”. We bear fruit no matter wherever God puts us.

3\. Good fruit doesn't rationalize the flesh as personality

The desires of the flesh, you dont know where they lead you

Galatians 5:16, 5:22 “desires of the flesh” “fruit of the spirit”

*You dont have patience because you dont trust God, you think you are in control of the ship.*

It isnt your personality, but you havent yielded to the spirit of God

4\. Good fruit is revealed under pressure

If you are not on the right road, you can always change lanes

5\. Good fruit is experienced in relationships

Do you know anybody different than you?

Where you are really tested is when you get around people who are different from you, can taste your fruit, and realize they need to get off of their flesh road

I have a God that I am supposed to honor. It matters what you say and do.

The people around you should be wondering where they got your fruit
