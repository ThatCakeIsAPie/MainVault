“Hermaneutics” the study of the bible in the context in which it is written

The gospels are written like a narrative

The gospels are not linear, but the epistles are more linear

Galatians 5:1 it is for freedom that Christ has set us free. Stand firm, then, and do not let yourselves be burdened again by a yoke of slavery

Freedom is not captivity, it doesnt mean we are held back from the pleasures in your life, freedom is how Christ designed us to live

There is a group of Christians that can come in through the church and not make the main thing the main thing, putting additional restrictions in place in order to be able to follow Jesus

We all have bad days. But when you have Christ in you, the fruit in your life comes out regardless of situation

1.  Freedom has an enemy: drift

It often isnt what you do wrong one time that puts your life into a mess. And it often isnt one thing you do right that gets it on track.

It is the small decisions we make daily. It is slow decisions made over a long period of time.

Drift is a result of complacency

When you become accustomed to that of God, it becomes normal. You can become desensitized to the workings of Jesus

What used to bother you doesnt bother you as much anymore, doesnt seem like that big of a deal

At one point, pornography used to seem like such a big thing to you. But over time, as you slip up, it doesnt bother you as much anymore

It is not one day you end up there, but the small decisions

2.  Fruit is the evidence of who is leading

When you have gifting, things you didnt work for or come up with, you can think you are producing fruit. But God gave those to you. But fruit is not something you can fake.

At some point, gifting will fail you

One can have all these things, but when God starts to deal with your heart, He starts to work on you

When you submit to the process and the pruning God is doing in your life, you will end up farther

*The last thing you want is to get what you want, but not have the character to sustain it.*

The fruit is just the byproduct

If people see the fruit of your life, and it isnt what people want, it isnt a condemnation, it is a secret invitation to come back to Jesus

3.  Walking in the Spirit is how you stay free

***Jesus died to set you free, not condemn you.***
