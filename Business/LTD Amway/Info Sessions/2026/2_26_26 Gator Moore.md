This is a network marketing business. What is the first word? Network.

Networking is different than marketing. In network marketing, the prerequisite to marketing is to network.

A normal question for new people is “should I prioritize customers or sponsoring?” Customers dont recruit other customers. But new people can.

Get good at audios and reading. Get good at selling too, but really get good at networking.

5 Level to a Conversation:

A list is most effective, but smallest

B list, most people dont go through it

C list has the most but is least effective

Your greatest asset in this business is your ability to sponsor

You will have to start the process with about 12 in your c list to sponsor 1. The other methods are more effective, but this is infinite.

The note the song starts on is the note it ends on. Dont start it weird.

Ask a question where the answer will give you the ammunition for the next question

Ask them about what they do for work. People’s favorite topic is about themselves.

Make them feel comfortable and take a genuine interest in them.

If you are going to ask a question, have the next question prepared.

Common ground is the next step after initiating

Starting out, just go do the thing. After, you are networking for a reason. You want to tighten things up.

The next step after the first is establishing common ground. If you dont like them, you probably dont want to be in business with them. If they dont like you, they probably wont be responsive to a business opportunity.

There are better common grounds than others

Linking to someone in business career-wise

“I’m in business with engineers. I’ve found they usually are winners, but get stuck pretty early.”
