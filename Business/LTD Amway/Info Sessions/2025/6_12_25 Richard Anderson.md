<u>Mechanics of the 1st Meeting</u>

MG1, Meet and Greet 1, First Meeting, whatever you want to call it

1.  Intro

    1.  What we are doing here - Eliminate awkwardness

As quickly as possible, he moves into what we are doing here

He tries to recap and get into it, set the frame of the conversation

He wants to kill that rush

He wants to get their verbal permission to get into his story

2.  Short Story

    1.  My personal story / My mentors

    2.  *Who vs What* (The big one)

    3.  What they do

“I had to go through a process over the course of a few weeks to gain access to the mentorship”

“They don’t teach it to everybody”

“That’s why we are sitting down, i want to see if it even makes sense”

It’s not a course online or whatever

These people are teaching people how to retire in their early 30s

Might take you between 1-10 minutes depending on how good you are and how good your relationship with them is

If you are in a flow, they should feel an eagerness

3.  Get to know the person

    1.  Be interested and LISTEN

It doesn’t matter exactly how you do this, just care. Listen.

You are speaking to another one of God’s children

4.  Network Marketing

    1.  Be brief

“We do network marketing, have you ever heard of network marketing?”

If they say yes, try to undo some of that experience

Want to see their experience

Use examples of things they know

What they want to know is that it doesn’t take money to start

Emphasize that we are not looking for investors

5.  What we look for

    1.  Core values/things they will see at info

    2.  Qualify them by asking if they’d value learning more

Ask them if they are trustworthy

How does your calendar look like if you could do a meeting for 5 hours a week?

6.  End the meeting

    1.  Introduction to the book

He wants them to feel that this is not THE meeting

Make it feel like it isn’t any thing

Don’t hype it up and make it feel abnormal

Set yourself up for the REAL meeting

<u>How to Lead into the next Meeting (MG2/QI)</u>

- Research

He wants them going home feeling like this was normal

He wants to say things that remind them of normal stuff
