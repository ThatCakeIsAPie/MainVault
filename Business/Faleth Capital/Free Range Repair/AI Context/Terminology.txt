Total = The total cost to the customer after everything. Taxes, fees, subtotal, etc.
Subtotal = The total cost to the customer before taxes and fees.
Gross Profit = Subtotal - COG
COG = Cost of Goods
COA = Cost of Acquisition (Basically CAC, Cost to Acquire the Customer)
COF = Cost of Fulfillment (Comp plan set aside for all human labor)
Fixed Costs = Guaranteed monthly expenses
PCB = Project Completion Bonus (Paid out once the project is completed)
PC Royalties = Royalties paid out to the parent company
