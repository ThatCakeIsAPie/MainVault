The idea for compensation is to break down our business into its individual processes, attaching a point value to each process, and letting our associates fulfill the rest.

If one prefers to talk to customers and do light repair work on the side, and another prefers to just sit down and work, so be it. As long as the work gets done, we dont care who does it. We track contribution to align incentives and to know how much to pay who.

If a project has a total of 40 points and someone gets 8 points, they will keep 20% of the COF payout for that project. Just individual points / total points = share of payout.

Less processes means each point is worth more.

Additionally, a quarterly profit share is distributed to our associates. Share of the profit share is determined by the same system, in a unique way. We track everyone's earnings, total them together, and divide each person's earnings by the total. That is how we decide how much of the share everyone gets.

The more money the shop makes, the more money everyone makes.
