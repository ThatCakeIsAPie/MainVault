Our core offer comes from the understanding that our customers don't want a repair, they want their device to be restored to its pre-damaged state.

As a result, we don't offer "repairs" but a complete restoration. This means taking a refurbished device that we know works, swapping the boards from each device, so the customer gets a basically new device.

3 Offers:
- Refurbished device (75% of the price of a new device)
- Restoration (50% of the price of a new device)
- DIY Parts and Tools

If a new device of that model costs $800, we can offer a a refurb for $600 or a restoration for $400. If only the glass needed replaced, that might cost us $150. If the screen itself is good, we can get $20-100 on an LCD buyback.
