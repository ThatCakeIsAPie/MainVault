A win-win referral program based around a small, personalized business card.

The program itself is based on the number of referrals. Each referral gives a referral point.

1 point: Free screen protector + 2 months of insurance
2 points: Free case and screen protector + 4 months of insurance
3 points: Free restoration, case and screen protector + 6 months of insurance

The referred: Free screen protector + 4 total months of insurance on first restoration

There is no cap to how many points a referrer can receive.

Each business card has two sides: one for the referrer and one for the referred.
The side for the referred has a QR code with the referee's QR code printed on a blank square and their name above it. It also tells them what they can get by redeeming the card.
The other side has the referral program on it to remind the referrer what they can receive by handing them out. It also serves as a subtle nod to the referred what they can get if they too get a repair done and refer their friends.
