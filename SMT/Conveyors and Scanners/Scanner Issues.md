## Problem
1. The scanner is having difficulty scanning a QR code even when it is right below it

## Root Cause
1. The scanner needs tuned
	1. The scanner is seeing the QR code as either too bright/too dark
	2. The scanner is seeing the QR code as too blurry

## Solution
1. Tune the scanner by finding the "tune" button and holding it down until the green and red lights on the side go away
	1. The red flashlight should start to flicker
	2. If done correctly, the 5 lights on the side should start to become orange as the tuning process completes
	3. Once completed, the scanner should scan the QR code
